ðŸ“œ Our History

Founded in September 12, 2004, Kings Football Agency Sierra Leone was born from a passion to nurture local talent and provide a professional pathway to the international stage. Our founders, former professional players and sports management experts, recognized the immense potential in Sierra Leonean football that often went unnoticed by the global market.

Starting with just fifteen players, we have grown to become the nation's leading football agency, representing over 50 talented athletes across various age groups and positions. Our commitment to excellence and player development has earned us recognition both locally and internationally.

We've successfully facilitated transfers to clubs in Europe, Asia, and across Africa, while maintaining our core mission of developing Sierra Leone's football ecosystem.
